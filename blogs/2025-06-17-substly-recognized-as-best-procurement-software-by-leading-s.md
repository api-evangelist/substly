<h5 style="font-weight: normal;">Substly has officially been named one of the best procurement software by <a href="https://research.com/software/best-procurement-software">Research.com</a> for its intuitive, cost-efficient tools tailored to help small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) manage SaaS subscriptions, vendors, and spend without complexity.</h5> 
<p>Research.com is well known for assessing platforms using expert analysis, user feedback ratings, and overall market performance. This recognition confirms Substly as a valuable tool for SMBs looking to reduce costs, boost security, and gain clear insights. Unlike enterprise-focused platforms, Substly is designed specifically for the needs of growing businesses. It makes software procurement, shadow IT, and license management easier for smaller teams with limited resources.</p>
