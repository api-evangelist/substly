<p style="font-weight: bold;">Key Takeaways</p> 
<ul> 
 <li style="font-weight: normal;"><strong>Vertical SaaS:</strong> Tailored for specific industries with specialized features (e.g., healthcare or life sciences), offering better alignment with industry needs but limited flexibility across other sectors.</li> 
 <li style="font-weight: normal;"><strong>Horizontal SaaS:&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;Broad solutions like CRMs that cater to multiple industries, providing scalability and versatility but sometimes lacking niche functionalities.</li> 
 <li style="font-weight: normal;"><strong>Choosing the right model:&nbsp;</strong>Businesses often benefit from a mix of both Vertical and Horizontal SaaS, balancing specialized tools with adaptable, cross-functional solutions.</li> 
</ul>
